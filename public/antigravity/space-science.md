# Intro to Space Science

Space science encompasses all of the scientific disciplines that involve space exploration and study natural phenomena and physical bodies occurring in outer space.

## Orbital Mechanics
Orbital mechanics or astrodynamics is the application of ballistics and celestial mechanics to the practical problems concerning the motion of rockets and other spacecraft.

### Key Concepts
- **Orbit:** The gravitationally curved trajectory of an object, such as the trajectory of a planet around a star or a natural satellite around a planet.
- **Escape Velocity:** The minimum speed needed for a free, non-propelled object to escape from the gravitational influence of a massive body.

## Space Environment
The space environment is harsh and poses significant challenges for exploration.
- **Microgravity:** The condition in which people or objects appear to be weightless.
- **Radiation:** Space is filled with ionizing radiation, which can be harmful to living organisms and electronic equipment.
- **Vacuum:** Space is a near-perfect vacuum, meaning there is very little matter.
