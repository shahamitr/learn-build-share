# Basic Prompting Strategies

Prompting is the art of communicating with LLMs. A well-written prompt ensures that the model provides accurate, relevant, and useful responses.

## Key Strategies

### 1. Be Clear and Concise
Avoid ambiguity and provide specific instructions.

### 2. Provide Context
Give the model background information to help it understand the task.

### 3. Use Examples
Show the model what you want it to do by providing examples.

### 4. Break Down Complex Tasks
Divide large tasks into smaller, more manageable steps.

### 5. Iterate and Refine
Experiment with different prompts and refine them based on the model's output.
