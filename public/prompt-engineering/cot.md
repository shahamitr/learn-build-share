# Chain-of-Thought Prompting

Chain-of-thought prompting is a powerful technique where you encourage the model to break down complex problems step-by-step.

## Key Concepts

- **Reasoning:** Show the model how to think through a problem.
- **Intermediate Steps:** Break down the task into smaller, more manageable steps.
- **Logical Flow:** Ensure the model's reasoning is clear and easy to follow.
- **Self-Correction:** Encourage the model to check its own work.

## Best Practices

- **Step-by-Step Instructions:** Use clear, step-by-step instructions.
- **Examples of Reasoning:** Provide examples of how to think through a problem.
- **Logical Connectors:** Use words like "first," "then," "finally," etc.
- **Verification:** Ask the model to verify its own reasoning.
