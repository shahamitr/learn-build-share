# GitLab CI/CD Basics

GitLab CI/CD is a powerful tool for automating your development workflow. Understanding how to define pipelines and jobs is essential for building scalable and maintainable applications.

## Key Concepts

- **Pipelines:** Automate your development workflow using GitLab pipelines.
- **Jobs:** Define specific tasks for your pipelines.
- **Stages:** Organize your jobs into logical stages.
- **Runners:** Execute your jobs on GitLab runners.

## Best Practices

- **Use a Consistent CI/CD Strategy:** Use a consistent CI/CD strategy for your GitLab projects.
- **Follow the Documentation:** Follow the GitLab documentation for best results.
- **Use a Consistent Style:** Use a consistent coding style for your project.
- **Test Your CI/CD Setup:** Test your CI/CD setup to ensure it works correctly.
