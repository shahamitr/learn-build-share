# Concepts Related to "Antigravity"

In this module, we'll explore the theoretical concepts related to "antigravity."

## Negative Energy (Theoretical)
Negative energy is a concept used in physics to explain the nature of certain fields, such as the gravitational field.

## Exotic Matter
Exotic matter is any kind of non-baryonic matter—that is, matter that is not made of protons, neutrons, and electrons.

## Vacuum Energy / Zero-Point Energy
Vacuum energy is an underlying background energy that exists in space throughout the entire universe.

## Gravitational Fields vs. Acceleration
The principle of equivalence states that the effects of gravity and acceleration are indistinguishable.

## Practical Tip
When thinking about antigravity, remember that it's still mostly theoretical; we haven't yet found a way to create or control it.
