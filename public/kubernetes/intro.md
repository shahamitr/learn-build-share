# Introduction to Kubernetes

Kubernetes (also known as K8s) is an open-source system for automating deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.

## Key Features

- **Container Orchestration**: Automates the deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.
- **Service Discovery**: Automatically discovers and connects services.
- **Load Balancing**: Automatically balances traffic across containers.
- **Self-Healing**: Automatically restarts failed containers.

## Why Kubernetes?

Kubernetes is a great choice for managing large-scale containerized applications. It's a powerful tool for building anything from simple microservices to complex enterprise applications.
