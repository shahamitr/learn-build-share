# DevOps Awareness & Leadership

In this module, we'll explore the role of QA in DevOps and leadership.

## CI/CD Awareness
- **Continuous Integration Basics:** Understanding how CI works and the role of QA in CI.
- **Tools Awareness:** Jenkins (conceptual), Git basics.
- **Continuous Deployment Basics:** Understanding how CD works and the role of QA in CD.

## Advanced API Testing
- **Authentication (Token, OAuth Basics):** Understanding how to test authenticated APIs.
- **JSON Validation:** Verifying that the response body is correct.
- **API Chaining:** Testing a sequence of API calls.

## Documentation & Leadership
- **Test Metrics & Reporting:** Measuring and reporting on the quality of the software.
- **Mentoring Junior Testers:** Sharing knowledge and helping others grow.
- **Stakeholder Communication:** Communicating with developers, product owners, and other stakeholders.

## Practical Tip
In DevOps, QA is integrated into the entire development process.
