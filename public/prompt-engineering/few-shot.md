# Few-Shot Prompting

Few-shot prompting is a powerful technique where you provide the model with a few examples of the task you want it to perform.

## Key Concepts

- **Examples:** Provide 1-5 examples of input-output pairs.
- **Format:** Use a consistent format for the examples.
- **Style:** Show the model the desired style and tone.
- **Context:** Help the model understand the task's constraints.

## Best Practices

- **Diverse Examples:** Provide examples that cover different scenarios.
- **Clear Separation:** Use labels or delimiters to separate examples.
- **Relevant Examples:** Ensure the examples are directly related to the task.
- **Balanced Examples:** Avoid biasing the model towards a specific outcome.
