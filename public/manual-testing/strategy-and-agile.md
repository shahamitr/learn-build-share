# Test Strategy & Agile QA

In this module, we'll explore the strategic and collaborative aspects of software testing.

## Test Strategy & Planning
- **Creating Test Plans:** Documenting the objectives, resources, and schedule for a testing project.
- **Risk-based Testing:** Prioritizing testing based on the risk of failure.
- **Estimation Techniques:** Estimating the time and effort required for testing.

## Agile & Modern QA
- **Agile Methodology (Scrum):** Understanding how Agile works and the role of QA in Agile.
- **Sprint Planning, Daily Standups:** Participating in Agile ceremonies.
- **Role of QA in Agile:** Ensuring quality throughout the development process.
- **Writing Test Cases from User Stories:** Translating user requirements into testable scenarios.

## Practical Tip
In Agile, QA is a team effort. Everyone is responsible for quality.
