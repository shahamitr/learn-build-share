# GitLab Runners

GitLab runners are the execution engines for your GitLab CI/CD pipelines. Understanding how to register and manage runners is essential for building scalable and maintainable applications.

## Key Concepts

- **Registration:** Register your runners with GitLab.
- **Tags:** Tag your runners for specific jobs.
- **Executors:** Choose the right executor for your runners (e.g., Shell, Docker, Kubernetes).
- **Monitoring:** Monitor your runners for performance and health.

## Best Practices

- **Use a Consistent Runner Strategy:** Use a consistent runner strategy for your GitLab projects.
- **Follow the Documentation:** Follow the GitLab documentation for best results.
- **Use a Consistent Style:** Use a consistent coding style for your project.
- **Test Your Runner Setup:** Test your runner setup to ensure it works correctly.
